

# Vizuálna identita Popri.cz - Kompletný branding prompt

## Prehľad

Pripravím vám **univerzálny branding prompt** (v angličtine pre najlepšie výsledky v AI generátoroch), ktorý môžete použiť pre tvorbu všetkých marketingových materiálov v jednotnom štýle webu.

---

## Farebná paleta

| Názov | HEX kód | Použitie |
|-------|---------|----------|
| **Noir (pozadie)** | `#0A0A0A` | Hlavné pozadie všetkých materiálov |
| **Noir Light** | `#121212` | Karty, panely, sekundárne pozadie |
| **Noir Lighter** | `#1F1F1F` | Jemné odtiene, bordery |
| **Gold (primárna)** | `#D4A517` | Nadpisy, CTA tlačidlá, akcenty |
| **Gold Soft** | `#E5B84A` | Jemnejšie zlaté efekty, glow |
| **Cream (text)** | `#F5F0E8` | Hlavný text, body copy |
| **Cream Light** | `#FAF8F5` | Svetlejší text, popisky |

---

## Typografia

| Typ | Font | Štýl | Použitie |
|-----|------|------|----------|
| **Nadpisy (H1-H3)** | Playfair Display | Bold/SemiBold | Hlavné titulky, headline |
| **Podnadpisy (H4-H6)** | Playfair Display | Medium/Regular | Sekundárne titulky |
| **Body text** | Inter | Regular/Medium | Odstavce, popisy |
| **CTA tlačidlá** | Inter | SemiBold | Akčné prvky |

## Univerzálny branding prompt

```text
VISUAL BRAND IDENTITY: Popri.cz - Luxury Noir + Gold Editorial Design

STYLE:
- Premium luxury editorial design inspired by high-end fashion magazines
- Sophisticated, modern, trustworthy aesthetic
- Award-winning web design quality (Awwwards/FWA level)

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary background: Deep noir black #0A0A0A
- Secondary background: Charcoal #121212 (for cards/panels)
- Primary accent: Rich warm gold #D4A517 (for headlines, CTAs, icons)
- Secondary accent: Soft gold #E5B84A (for glow effects, highlights)
- Text color: Warm cream #F5F0E8 (main text)
- Border color: Dark gray #1F1F1F with gold accents on hover

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Headlines: Elegant serif font (Playfair Display style) - bold, tracking tight
- Body text: Clean modern sans-serif (Inter style) - regular weight
- Numbers/Prices: Bold serif with gold gradient effect
- All headlines can use gold gradient: from #D4A517 to #B8860B

VISUAL EFFECTS:
- Glassmorphism: Semi-transparent panels with backdrop blur
- Gold glow: Subtle golden shadow around important elements (box-shadow: 0 0 30px rgba(212,165,23,0.3))
- Animated underlines on links (gold color)
- Magnetic hover effects (slight lift -4px on hover)
- Ken Burns subtle zoom on background images

DESIGN ELEMENTS:
- Rounded corners: 12px (0.75rem) on all cards and buttons
- Borders: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1) with gold on hover
- Shadows: Soft, dark with occasional gold glow for CTAs
- Icons: Line-style, cream colored with gold accents
- Fiber optic light trails: Thin golden lines representing fast internet

MOOD & FEEL:
- Premium and exclusive (like luxury brand marketing)
- Trustworthy and professional (technology service provider)
- Modern and sophisticated (not cheap or generic)
- Warm and welcoming (not cold or corporate)

BRAND INFO:
- Company: Popri.cz (authorized PODA partner)
- Service: Gigabit fiber internet + IPTV
- Region: Ostrava, Czech Republic
- Phone: 730 431 313

CRITICAL LANGUAGE REQUIREMENT:
All visible text in generated images MUST be in CZECH language.
If input is in Slovak or English, translate to Czech.
Use Czech: "Jak" (not "Ako"), "Rychlost" (not "Rýchlosť"), "Připojení" (not "Pripojenie").
Proper Czech diacritics: č, š, ž, ř, ď, ť, ň, ů, ý, á, í, é

NO: Watermarks, stock photo feel, generic designs, cluttered layouts
YES: Clean, editorial, magazine-quality, premium aesthetic
```

## Vizuálne elementy

### Logo štýl
- **popri** = zlatá farba (#D4A517), Playfair Display, bold
- **.cz** = krémová farba (#F5F0E8), Playfair Display, regular
- Podtitul "Autorizovaný partner PODA" = krémová, Inter, small caps, letter-spacing

### Ikonografia
- Štýl: Line icons (tenké linky)
- Farba: Krémová (#F5F0E8) s možným zlatým akcentom
- Sada: Router, WiFi signál, TV, rýchlosť, telefón, check mark

### Fotografie
- Štýl: Tmavá atmosféra s teplým zlatým svetlom
- Subjekty: Moderné interiéry, spokojené rodiny, home office
- Post-processing: Tmavšie tiene, zlaté highlights, filmový look
